Dessert Preferences

In the event that you are trying to surprise me with food, like a cake, and want to minimize the chance that you buy me something I can't or won't eat, this page may be helpful.

I will generally eat a wide variety of desserts. However, there are some exceptions. I might not eat desserts that contain any of the following:

  • Carrot cake or any other food containing nuts or seeds
  • Cream cheese frosting
  • Caramel

(Note that I do not have any known allergies. These are simply ingredients that may cause me to gag when used in desserts.)

I would also discourage you from choosing anything containing any of the following ingredients, which I will eat, but may not especially enjoy:

  • Blueberries
  • Apricots
  • Pears
  • Coffee
  • Alcohol-related flavors
  • Pumpkin

If you want something that I am specifically highly likely to enjoy, I would encourage you to consider food that uses a subset of these flavors:

  • Chocolate
  • Vanilla
  • Berries
  • Fruits

You are never under any obligation to try to accomodate my food preferences. However, I always appreciate it when people do!
